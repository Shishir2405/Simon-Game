INSTRUCTION

  • Click the "Start" button to initiate the game.
  • Simon will illuminate a sequence of colors. Pay close attention to the order of the colors.
  • Your turn! Click on the colored buttons in the exact order as shown by Simon.
  • Each correct sequence advances you to the next level, adding more challenge and fun.
  • Don't forget to listen! Simon plays a sound when game start and provide a visual cues.
  • Challenge yourself to reach higher levels and beat your own high score!
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends, and you can press "Start" to restart.
  • Enjoy the rhythmic patterns and improve your memory and concentration skills.
  • Ready to test your memory? Press "Start" and let the Simon Game begin!
